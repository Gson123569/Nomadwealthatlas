What is the Escape Tier?

You've built meaningful wealth. Now it's time to optimize.

The Escape tier is for individuals and families with $1M to $5M in net worth who are ready to take control of their tax situation and explore location-independent living.

At this level, you have access to a wide range of strategies that can significantly reduce your tax burden while maintaining flexibility and quality of life.

Key characteristics: you're likely still working (remotely or as an entrepreneur), you're open to relocation, and you're looking for proven, low-risk strategies.

Typical Profile

💼 Remote worker or entrepreneur
🏠 Open to relocation
📈 Growing investment portfolio
🎯 Focus on tax reduction
⏱️ 3-6 month implementation

Core Strategies

Proven approaches for the Escape tier.

1

Digital Nomad Visas

Leverage remote work visa programs. Countries like Portugal, Spain, Costa Rica, and Estonia offer 1-2 year visas with favorable tax treatment.

2

Tax-Advantaged Accounts

Maximize contributions to tax-advantaged accounts in your home country before departure. Consider Roth conversions during low-income years.

3

Offshore Banking

Establish basic offshore banking relationships in stable jurisdictions. Singapore, Switzerland, and Liechtenstein offer excellent options.

4

Geo-Arbitrage Living

Take advantage of lower cost-of-living destinations while earning in strong currencies. Popular: Portugal, Mexico, Thailand, Colombia.

5

Investment Optimization

Restructure your portfolio for tax efficiency. Consider Ireland-domiciled ETFs for European residents.

6

Residency Planning

Establish tax residency in favorable jurisdictions. Many countries offer special regimes for new residents.
