We're Building the Map We Wish We Had

The Nomad Wealth Atlas started with a simple question: Why is information about global wealth structuring so fragmented, expensive, and inaccessible?

Our Story

It began in a co-working space in Lisbon, 2019.

Three of us — a developer from Canada, a tax lawyer from Germany, and an entrepreneur from Australia — kept running into the same problem. We were all trying to optimize our tax situations, protect our assets, and build location-independent lives.

But the information we needed was scattered across expensive consultant reports, outdated blog posts, and forum threads full of conflicting advice.

So we decided to build what we wished existed: a comprehensive, always-current, freely accessible guide to global wealth structuring.
