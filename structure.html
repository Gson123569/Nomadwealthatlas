What is the Structure Tier?

You've escaped the rat race. Now it's time to build your fortress.

The Structure tier is for individuals and families with $5M to $20M in net worth who are ready to implement more sophisticated wealth management strategies.

You're no longer just optimizing personal tax; you're structuring businesses, navigating tax treaties, and building a multi-jurisdictional foundation.

Key characteristics: you likely have business interests or significant investment income, you're comfortable with multi-year planning horizons.

Typical Profile

🏢Business owner or serial entrepreneur
📊Diversified investment portfolio
🌍Multi-jurisdictional considerations
🎯Focus on structure optimization
⏱️6-12 month implementation

Core Strategies

Proven approaches for the Structure tier.

1

Company Formation

Establish holding companies in favorable jurisdictions. Cyprus, Malta, and Netherlands offer attractive holding company regimes.

2

Tax Treaty Optimization

Leverage double tax treaties to minimize withholding taxes. Strategic placement of intermediate holding companies unlocks savings.

3

IP Structuring

Centralize intellectual property in low-tax jurisdictions. Ireland, Switzerland, and Singapore offer favorable IP regimes.

4

Residency Optimization

Strategic residency planning becomes critical. Consider Cyprus non-dom, Malta's remittance basis, or Italy's flat-tax.

5

Trust Foundations

Establish simple trust or foundation structures for asset protection. Cayman, BVI, and Liechtenstein offer flexible options.

6

Banking Diversification

Build a diversified banking network across multiple jurisdictions for wealth preservation and business operations.
