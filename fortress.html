What is the Fortress Tier?

Your wealth is significant. Protection is everything.

The Fortress tier is for individuals and families with $50M to $100M in net worth where asset protection becomes the primary concern.

At this level, you're a target. Frivolous lawsuits, political risk, and family disputes can threaten everything you've built. Your structures must be bulletproof.

Key characteristics: you have significant public exposure, you need maximum privacy, and you're willing to pay for the best protection available.

Typical Profile

🛡️Asset protection priority
🔒Maximum privacy required
⚖️Litigation risk exposure
🎯Multi-jurisdictional structures
⏱️18-24 month implementation

Core Strategies

Proven approaches for the Fortress tier.

1

Multi-Jurisdictional Structures

Create layered structures across multiple jurisdictions. Each layer serves a specific purpose and adds protection.

2

Advanced Trusts

Implement complex trust structures including purpose trusts, STAR trusts, and reserved powers trusts.

3

Privacy Planning

Minimize public visibility of assets through nominee arrangements, private trust companies, and bearer instruments where legal.

4

Insurance Wrappers

Use private placement life insurance (PPLI) and variable annuities to hold investments with tax deferral and creditor protection.

5

Pre-Immigration Planning

Structure assets before moving to high-tax jurisdictions to minimize future tax exposure.

6

Exit Strategies

Develop clear exit plans for every jurisdiction in case political or legal conditions change.
