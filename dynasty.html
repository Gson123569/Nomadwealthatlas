What is the Dynasty Tier?

You're building something that will outlast you.

The Dynasty tier is for individuals and families with $20M to $50M in net worth who are thinking beyond their own lifetime.

At this level, the focus shifts from personal optimization to multi-generational planning. You're building structures that will protect and grow your wealth for your children and grandchildren.

Key characteristics: you have complex family dynamics to consider, you're thinking about succession planning, and you're willing to invest in sophisticated structures.

Core Strategies

Proven approaches for the Dynasty tier.

1

Family Office Setup

Establish a single-family office to coordinate investments, tax planning, and family governance across generations.

2

Dynasty Trusts

Create perpetual trusts that can last for generations. Jurisdictions like South Dakota and Wyoming allow perpetual trusts.

3

Private Foundations

Establish private foundations in favorable jurisdictions. Liechtenstein and Panama offer flexible foundation structures.

4

Succession Planning

Develop comprehensive succession plans that address both wealth transfer and family governance.

5

Philanthropic Vehicles

Create charitable foundations or donor-advised funds to align wealth with values and optimize tax benefits.

6

Asset Protection

Implement advanced asset protection strategies including insurance wrappers and segregated portfolios.
