Data Quality & Limitations

Important context for interpreting the information in the Atlas.

What We Do Well

✅ Tax Rate Comparisons

Our tax rate data is verified against official sources and updated within 30 days of changes.

✅ Residency Requirements

Program requirements are sourced directly from official government publications.

✅ Cost of Living Trends

Relative cost comparisons are accurate; absolute numbers vary by lifestyle.

Known Limitations

⚠️ Individual Circumstances

Tax liability varies significantly based on individual circumstances we cannot account for.

⚠️ Rapid Changes

Tax laws can change with minimal notice. Always verify current rules before acting.

⚠️ Simplified Models

Our calculators use simplified models. Real-world tax planning is more complex.
